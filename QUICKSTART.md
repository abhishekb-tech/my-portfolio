# 🚀 Quick Start - Deploy Your Portfolio in 5 Minutes

## Fastest Way to Deploy (No CLI needed!)

### Step 1: Build Your Project
```bash
cd portfolio
npm run build
```

### Step 2: Deploy to Netlify
1. Go to **[https://app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)**
2. **Drag and drop** the `dist` folder
3. Done! Your site is live! 🎉

### Step 3: Customize Your URL (Optional)
1. Click on your site in Netlify dashboard
2. Go to **Site settings** → **Site details**
3. Click **Change site name**
4. Enter something like: `abhishek-bhatt-portfolio`
5. Your URL becomes: `abhishek-bhatt-portfolio.netlify.app`

---

## Add to LinkedIn

1. Copy your Netlify URL
2. Go to your **LinkedIn profile**
3. Click **"Add profile section"** → **"Featured"**
4. Click **"Add featured"** → Choose **"Link"**
5. Paste your portfolio URL
6. Add title: "My Portfolio Website"
7. Save!
